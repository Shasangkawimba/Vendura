# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Managers, Finance, and Directors who are responsible for reviewing, approving, and tracking vendor contracts and compliance. They use this application internally, often during busy workdays where quick scanning and decision-making are critical.

## Product Purpose
Vendura is an internal Vendor Contract & Compliance Management System. Its core purpose is to digitize and enforce a strict contract approval workflow (Manager → Finance → Director), maintain an immutable history of decisions, manage document versioning, and track vendor compliance. Success looks like an error-free, auditable process where users can instantly understand a contract's status without confusion.

## Brand Personality
Professional, Trustworthy, Clean.
The tone is highly credible and reliable, prioritizing function over flash. Emulating the visual aesthetics of Linear, Notion, and the Stripe Dashboard—utilizing clean layouts, minimal chrome, bold typography, and a refined dark mode. It should evoke confidence and enterprise-grade security while remaining highly approachable.

## Anti-references
- No flashy consumer app aesthetics (e.g., Jenius, Livin).
- No saturated, bright colors or excessive gradients; seriousness is prioritized over fun.
- No heavy Glassmorphism/Neumorphism that obscures legibility.
- No playful 3D or cartoon illustrations common in SaaS marketing pages.
- No slow, decorative animations or excessive transitions that block the workflow.
- Avoid cluttered interfaces (like Bloomberg Terminal); it must remain easily scannable.

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity Before Aesthetics:** Status indicators and crucial data points must be unmistakable and unambiguous.
2. **Restraint and Focus:** Eliminate non-essential visual noise to reduce cognitive load during contract reviews.
3. **Scannability First:** Use spacing, typography, and clear grid alignments to allow rapid reading of complex information.
4. **Dependable Feedback:** System actions (approvals, rejections) must feel solid and instantaneous.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Mandatory support for both Dark mode and Light mode to accommodate any working environment.
- High contrast requirements strictly enforced for status badges (e.g., distinct colors for DRAFT, WAITING, ACTIVE, REJECTED) to avoid ambiguity.
- High standard typographic readability with a minimum body font size of 14px; never compromise legibility for compactness.
